Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I apply for the space mission?

A: You can apply through our online portal or by contacting our recruitment team.

Q: What are the requirements for the space mission?

A: Candidates must have a relevant degree and pass a series of physical and psychological tests.

Q: How long is the training program?

A: The training program lasts for 3 years and includes both theoretical and practical components.

Q: Do I need prior rocket-flying experience to "Apply!"?

A: None at all! If you can press big buttons and handle intense G-forces without spilling your juice box, you are prime explorer material.

Q: What should I pack for a mission?

A: Pack light. Bring a toothbrush, a change of clothes, and an unyielding sense of cosmic adventure. Leave your planetary baggage (and heavy furniture) at home.

Q: Are aliens friendly?

A: Mostly! If an alien offers you glowing green candy, standard protocol dictates that you smile, wave, and politely let your co-pilot taste-test it first.

Q: How do I return to Earth if I miss my flight?

A: You don't. Just kidding! (Or are we?) Our automated return pods run on a strict schedule, so don't get distracted chasing shooting stars past curfew.
